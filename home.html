Welcome to GFGC Dandeli

About Government First Grade College, Dandeli

Government First Grade College, Dandeli, established in 2013, is situated in the serene town of Dandeli, Uttar Kannada, Karnataka. Affiliated with Karnataka University, Dharwad, and accredited with a B+ grade by NAAC, the college is committed to providing quality education in Arts, Commerce, and Science. With a student strength of 429 and a dedicated faculty of 16 members, the institution offers undergraduate programs such as B.Com, BCA, BA, and B.Sc.

Our College Facilities

Library

Our college library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, e-books, and digital resources. Modern architecture and comfortable seating create a serene study environment, with regular workshops and reading events fostering a love for learning.

College Ground

The college ground is spacious, well-maintained, and ideal for sports such as cricket, football, and athletics. Annual meets and tournaments create a vibrant outdoor environment for students to socialize and stay active.

Classrooms

Spacious classrooms with smartboards, projectors, and modern tools create an interactive learning environment. Well-lit, ventilated, and ergonomically arranged, they encourage collaborative learning and practical education.

Computer Lab

Equipped with high-performance computers and latest software, students gain hands-on experience in programming, data analysis, and multimedia projects. The lab supports innovation, coding competitions, and practical learning.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

NSS instills social responsibility through community service and awareness programs. Activities such as health camps and environmental drives help students develop empathy, leadership, and teamwork.

NCC (National Cadet Corps)

NCC builds discipline, leadership, and patriotism through training, camps, and adventure activities. Cadets develop resilience, teamwork, and civic responsibility.
