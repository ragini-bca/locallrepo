Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A)

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A) course at Government First Grade College, Dandeli, aims to build a strong academic foundation in the core areas of computer science and software development. Students are introduced to subjects like Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python), Database Management, Web Technologies, Computer Networks, and Operating Systems.

This program emphasizes hands-on learning through projects, coding exercises, and mini-research work. Graduates can pursue careers in software engineering, web development, data analytics, or IT administration. B.C.A also opens a pathway for higher studies like M.C.A or M.Sc. in Computer Science.

Course Duration:

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

PUC / 10+2 or equivalent with Mathematics or Computer Science background preferred.
