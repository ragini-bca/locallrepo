Bachelor of Arts (B.A)

The Bachelor of Arts program provides students with a deep understanding of humanities, social sciences, and languages. Students can specialize in subjects like History, Political Science, Sociology, Economics, and English Literature. The program focuses on analytical thinking, communication, and critical writing.

The B.A curriculum encourages students to engage with society and culture through research, projects, and discussions. It helps develop problem-solving and leadership skills essential for roles in civil services, education, journalism, social work, and administration.

Course Duration:

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

PUC / 10+2 or equivalent in any stream.
