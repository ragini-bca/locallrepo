Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

The Bachelor of Commerce program equips students with strong foundations in accounting, finance, taxation, and business management. It focuses on developing managerial and analytical abilities essential for corporate and entrepreneurial careers.

The course includes subjects such as Financial Accounting, Business Law, Corporate Finance, Marketing, and Auditing. Graduates often pursue careers in accounting firms, banks, or business organizations, or continue with M.Com, MBA, or professional certifications like CA, CMA, or CS.

Course Duration:

3 Years (6 Semesters)
