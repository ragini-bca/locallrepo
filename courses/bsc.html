Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

The Bachelor of Science program focuses on scientific understanding, experimentation, and research. Students learn core concepts in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and Computer Science, depending on their chosen combination.

The B.Sc curriculum promotes practical learning through lab work and projects, helping students develop analytical, observational, and problem-solving abilities. Graduates can pursue careers in research, teaching, environmental studies, or continue with higher education in M.Sc or professional programs.

Course Duration:

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

PUC / 10+2 or equivalent with Science background.
