About Government First Grade College, Dandeli

Government First Grade College, Dandeli, established in 2013, is situated in the serene town of Dandeli, Uttar Kannada, Karnataka. Affiliated with Karnataka University, Dharwad and accredited with a B+ grade by NAAC, the college is committed to providing quality education in Arts, Commerce, and Science. With a student strength of 429 and a dedicated faculty of 16 members, the institution offers undergraduate programs such as B.Com, BCA, BA, and B.Sc., fostering academic excellence and holistic development for all students.

The college offers undergraduate programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. With a dedicated faculty and modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a rich library, and computer facilities, the institution emphasizes personalized attention and holistic development.

Beyond academics, the college actively encourages participation in cultural, sports, and community service activities through NSS and NCC programs, helping students build leadership, teamwork, and social responsibility. Regular workshops, seminars, and guest lectures provide exposure to contemporary trends and industry practices, preparing students for a successful career and lifelong learning. Government First Grade College, Dandeli, is a nurturing hub where knowledge, character, and innovation come together to shape the leaders of tomorrow.
